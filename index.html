About Mothu Properties

Mothu Properties isn't just about land — it's about legacy. We're a proudly South African business on a mission to empower families to secure their future through affordable land ownership. Our properties are situated in fast-developing, well-connected areas near schools, hospitals, malls, and public transport — because we believe where you live shapes how you live.

With us, you're not just buying land — you're investing in peace of mind, security, and the future of your loved ones. Our plots are competitively priced and build-ready, making them perfect for first-time homeowners, young families, and long-term investors alike. Every transaction is transparent, every document is verified, and every buyer is treated with care.

We make the journey to ownership simple with expert legal guidance, flexible payment plans starting from as little as R999/month, and one-on-one support from enquiry to key handover. Whether you're dreaming of your first home, a weekend retreat, or a long-term investment — Mothu Properties is your trusted partner every step of the way.

Over the years, we've helped dozens of South Africans turn open plots into homes, security, and generational assets. Our clients consistently share how simple, safe, and empowering the process was. With dedicated agents and ongoing support, you can start your journey with confidence — knowing you’re not just buying land, you're building your future with a team that cares.
